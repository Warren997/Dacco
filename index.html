Our Coop

HOW TO BECOME A MEMBER

Membership in BBCCC is open to the following: • Any natural person who is a citizen of the Philippines, of legal age and with the capacity to contract; • Any person at least 18 years of age with visible means

WHAT ARE THE DUTIES AND RIGHTS OF A MEMBER

Every member shall have the following duties: 1. Pay the installments on capital pledged as they fall due and participate in the capital build-up through regular savings;

BASIC SERVICES OFFERED BY DACCO

Basic Fixed Deposit/Share Capital (Minimum subscription of a member to the share capital is P5,000 which entitles the member to interest on his capital depending on the net surplus.)

Vision & Mission

Vision

To be a model cooperative that is responsive to the needs of its environment and where each member becomes: economically self-reliant; positive value oriented; and a socially responsible member of the community.

Mission

Lasting improvements in the quality of life of its members. A membership enlightened about cooperative values and their social responsibilities. Active involvement in community development.
